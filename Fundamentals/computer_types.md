# Computer Types

## Overview
- Different types of computers used in personal and enterprise networks.

## Types
- Laptops - Portable personal computer, focusing on mobility and battery life.
- Desktops - Fixed workstation computer, focusing on power and performance.
- Servers - Provides services and resources to other devices.
- Mobile devices - Focuses on portability.
- Embedded devices - May not connect to a network, just does its job inside the device (e.g. vending machine)

## Relevance
- Understanding computer types helps in support and troubleshooting.
