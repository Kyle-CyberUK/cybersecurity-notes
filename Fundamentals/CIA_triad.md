# CIA Triad

## Overview
- The CIA triad explains what cybersecurity protects, and how its three pillars define whether a system is secure.

## Key Concepts
- Confidentiality - Ensures sensitive data is accessible only to authorized users.
- Integrity - Ensures that data cannot be modified without authorization.
- Availability - Ensures systems and data are always accessible to authorized users when needed.

## Security Mindset
- The CIA triad is a framework used by security professionals to analyse incidents when they occur.
- Analysts may ask questions like:
  - Was sensitive data exposed?
  - Was data altered in any way?
  - Were systems unavailable?

## Relevance
The CIA triad is important because it forms the foundation of security architectures. It helps perform risk assessments, and develops policies to protect systems.
